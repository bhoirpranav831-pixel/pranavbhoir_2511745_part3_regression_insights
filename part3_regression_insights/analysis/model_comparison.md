## TASK 6 : COMPARE MODEL

three models were created to find out the factors that have a connection with monthly sales.

marketing spend model showed the coefficient of determination which equals 0.167, hence, this means that marketing spend can explain only a little part of variance of sales. Despite the fact that this factor is statistically significant, the model is not good enough.

footfall model showed much higher coefficient of determination – it was equal to 0.736. It means that customer footfall has a strong connection with monthly sales, and it is a good predictor while it is considered separately.

multiple regression model had the best result among all – it had the coefficient of determination of 0.811. In this model marketing spend, customer footfall, availability of stock, and dummy variables of regions are considered. All factors except dummy variable of North region are statistically significant. The model explains about 81.1% of variance of monthly sales, so it is chosen as the final one.

in general multiple regression model can give the best results from the business point of view.
