6. Why does regression show association but not automatically prove causation?

process of regression analysis only shows the statistical relationships among the variables it does not establish the fact that one variable is a cause of the other For instance although an increased number of customers is related to increased sales it is difficult to use the results of the analysis to determine whether an increase in the number of customers will lead to increased sales There could be other external variables affecting both the number of customers and sales.
